The Problem

Two unsolved problems. One program.

96% of small businesses plan to adopt AI — but most can't afford the talent to implement it. Meanwhile, recent graduates have nowhere to build real, verifiable AI experience. The tools are new enough that no one has a real track record — and every resume claims the same thing. Capstone.AI exists because both gaps are real, and they solve each other.

For Businesses

AI consultants charge $200–$500/hr. Hiring a full-time AI specialist means $120K+ before you know if it works. Most small businesses can't access AI capacity at any price that makes sense.

For Talent

Bootcamps and courses produce certificates. Employers want proof of real outcomes. Without client work, there's no portfolio — and without a portfolio, there's no first job.

What We Do

Three services. Real outcomes.

Each service is built around a defined scope, a standard toolset, and a measurable outcome benchmark. You see exactly what you're getting before you sign up.

Service 01

Customer Acquisition & Marketing

You need more customers but don't have time to write, post, and follow up consistently. We build an AI-assisted content and outreach system — built for your voice, your audience, and your goals — that keeps running after we're done.

How it works
  1. We learn your business, your customers, and what's worked before
  2. We build and deploy a content and outreach system using standard AI tools
  3. We hand off everything — templates, prompts, workflows — so you can run it yourself
Service 02

Content & Communications at Scale

You have things to say but can't produce content fast enough — for your website, email list, social, or proposals. We build a consistent content operation around your voice — not generic AI output.

How it works
  1. We extract your voice, tone, and what makes your business distinct
  2. We build a content system — templates, formats, and an AI workflow tailored to your channels
  3. We deliver a complete handoff kit so content production doesn't stop when we do
Service 03

Data Analysis & Business Intelligence

You have customer, sales, or operational data — but no clear picture of what it's telling you. We build a dashboard or analysis that answers the questions you actually have: who your best customers are, where you're losing money, what's working.

How it works
  1. We identify the 2–3 questions your data should be answering but isn't
  2. We build a clean analysis using standard tools (Google Looker Studio, Sheets, or equivalent)
  3. We document how to read it and update it — so it stays useful after delivery
